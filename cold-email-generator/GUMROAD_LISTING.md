## Full Product Description

### Write cold emails in 60 seconds. Send more. Land more clients.

Cold emailing is still one of the highest-ROI ways to get clients, jobs, or partnerships — but writing emails that don't sound like templates is hard and slow.

This tool fixes that. Fill in 6 fields about yourself and your target, hit Generate, and get 3 short, personalized cold emails ready to copy and send. Each one has a subject line, a personalized opening, your pitch, and a clear call to action.

**No subscriptions. No monthly fees. One download, yours forever.**

- Clean, modern browser interface
- AI-powered email generation (Claude AI model)

- 1–3 email variations per generation
- One-click copy for each email
- Works locally on your computer (free to run)

### How It Works

**Step 1** — Fill in 6 fields: your name, service, value proposition, their name, company, and pain point.

**Step 2** — Click Generate. The AI writes 3 short, personalized emails in about 5 seconds.

**Step 3** — Copy the best variation, tweak if you want, and send.
