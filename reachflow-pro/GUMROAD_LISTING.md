## What's Included

**Cold Email Generator**
Paste in your offer and a few details about your prospect and get a short, personalized cold email — complete with subject line, opener, pitch, and call to action. Built for replies, not impressions.

**Follow-up Sequence Builder**
Most replies come from follow-ups, not first emails. Tell it your situation and it builds a complete multi-touch sequence, timed and varied so your follow-ups don't feel copy-pasted.

**LinkedIn Outreach**
Generate connection requests and DMs calibrated for LinkedIn — short, personal, and nothing like the spam people ignore. Lower competition, higher reply rates.

**Objection Crusher**
Got a "not right now" or "we already have someone"? Paste the objection in and get a confident, non-pushy response that keeps the door open without sounding desperate.

**Subject Line Lab**
Enter your topic and audience and get a set of subject lines ranging from curiosity-driven to direct to benefit-led. Better subject lines mean more opens — test a few and find your winner.

**Opener Generator**
The first sentence is what kills most cold emails. Use this to generate personalized openers based on what you know about the prospect. Drop them into any email or message instantly.

## FAQ

**Is there a monthly fee?**
No. ReachFlow Pro is a one-time purchase. You pay once and own it outright. There are no subscription fees, renewal costs, or locked features.

**Do I have to pay for the AI?**
No. ReachFlow Pro uses Groq's free API tier. You get a free account at console.groq.com — no credit card required — and that's all you need. The free tier includes 14,400 requests per day, which is far more than any realistic outreach volume.

**What operating systems does it work on?**
Mac and Windows. Both are fully supported. The download includes a one-click launch script for each platform.

**Do I need to know how to code?**
No. The setup guide walks through every step in plain English. If you can download a file and follow instructions, you can run ReachFlow Pro.

**Where does my data go?**
Nowhere except Groq's API (to generate the content). The app runs on your own machine. Your prospect details and generated content are never stored on any server other than Groq's API call in the moment.

**What if I already have ReachFlow (the basic version)?**
ReachFlow Pro is a separate product with five additional tools and a more complete outreach workflow. It runs on port 5001, so both can coexist on the same machine.
